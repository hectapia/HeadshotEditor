# Professional Headshot Editor

A browser-based image editing tool designed to help you create professional headshots for LinkedIn, resumes, business cards, and other professional materials.

## üéØ Features

- **Drag & Drop Upload** - Easy image uploading with drag and drop support
- **Lighting Adjustments** - Fine-tune brightness, contrast, and saturation
- **Smart Cropping** - Zoom and position controls to focus on your face
- **Professional Backgrounds** - 8 neutral background colors to choose from
- **Instant Preview** - See changes in real-time as you adjust

## üöÄ Getting Started

### Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- An image file (JPG, PNG, or other common formats)

### How to Use

1. **Upload Your Photo**
   - Click "Choose Image" button, or
   - Drag and drop your image onto the upload area

2. **Adjust Lighting**
   - **Brightness** (-50 to +50): Lighten or darken the image
   - **Contrast** (50% to 200%): Adjust the difference between light and dark areas
   - **Saturation** (50% to 150%): Control color intensity

3. **Crop and Position**
   - **Zoom** (100% to 200%): Zoom in to focus on your face and remove background elements
   - **Vertical Position**: Move the image up or down
   - **Horizontal Position**: Move the image left or right

4. **Select Background**
   - Click on any of the 8 professional background colors
   - Options include: Light Gray, White, Soft Gray, Dark Blue, Slate, Charcoal, Cloud, and Professional Blue

5. **Download**
   - Click the "üíæ Download" button to save your professional headshot
   - File will be saved as `professional-headshot.png`

## üí° Tips for Best Results

### Optimal Settings
- **Zoom**: 110-140% to focus on face and shoulders
- **Background**: Light gray (#f5f5f5) or white for most professional settings
- **Brightness**: Adjust to ensure your face is well-lit
- **Contrast**: Increase slightly (105-115%) for sharper features

### Photography Best Practices
- Use a photo with good natural lighting
- Face the camera directly with a neutral or slight smile
- Wear professional attire appropriate for your industry
- Ensure the original photo is high resolution
- Remove glasses glare if possible in the original photo

### Background Selection Guide
- **Light Gray/White**: Universal professional choice, works with any industry
- **Dark Blue/Charcoal**: Corporate, finance, law, executive positions
- **Professional Blue**: Tech, creative, modern companies
- **Soft Gray/Cloud**: Consulting, healthcare, education

## ‚öôÔ∏è Controls Reference

### Brightness Control
- **Range**: -50 to +50
- **Default**: 0
- **Purpose**: Adjusts overall image lightness
- **Tip**: Use +10 to +20 for slightly underexposed photos

### Contrast Control
- **Range**: 50% to 200%
- **Default**: 100%
- **Purpose**: Adjusts difference between light and dark areas
- **Tip**: Increase to 110-120% for sharper, more defined features

### Saturation Control
- **Range**: 50% to 150%
- **Default**: 100%
- **Purpose**: Controls color intensity
- **Tip**: Reduce to 90-95% for a more subdued, professional look

### Zoom Control
- **Range**: 100% to 200%
- **Default**: 100%
- **Purpose**: Crops and focuses on subject
- **Tip**: Use 120-140% to remove unwanted background elements

### Position Controls
- **Range**: -100 to +100
- **Default**: 0
- **Purpose**: Fine-tune image placement after zooming
- **Tip**: Center your face in the frame after zooming

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### Image Won't Upload
- Ensure file is a valid image format (JPG, PNG, etc.)
- Check file size is reasonable (under 10MB recommended)
- Try a different browser

### Image Looks Distorted
- Use the Reset All button to start over
- Try zooming out to 100% first, then gradually increase
- Ensure original photo has good resolution

### Background Doesn't Change
- Click directly on the background color swatch
- Check that image has loaded properly
- Try refreshing the page

### Download Doesn't Work
- Check browser permissions for downloads
- Ensure pop-up blocker isn't interfering
- Try right-clicking the download button
